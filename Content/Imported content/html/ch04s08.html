4.8. Example: user management in a health system

In a health system, users are logically grouped with respect to the task they perform and the position they occupy.

  1. Define which users that should have the role as system administrators. They are often part of the national HIS division and should have full authority in the system.

  2. Create roughly one user role for each position.
