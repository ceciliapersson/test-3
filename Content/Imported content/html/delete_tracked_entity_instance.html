25.17. Delete a TEI

Warning

When you delete a TEI, you delete all data associated with the TEI.

  1. Open the Tracker Capture app.

  2. Open an existing TEI dashboard.

  3. In the top right corner, click the button > Delete.

  4. Click Yes to confirm.
